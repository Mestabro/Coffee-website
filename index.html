Espresso

Taste Profile:

Rich, bold, and intense with a thick crema. Features deep, concentrated flavors with slight bitterness and a strong aroma.

Strength: Very Strong

Americano

Taste Profile:

Smooth and mellow with espresso's richness diluted by hot water. Less intense but retains a full coffee flavor with subtle bitterness.

Strength: Medium-Strong

Cappuccino

Taste Profile:

Creamy and balanced with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam. Smooth texture with a perfect balance of coffee and milk sweetness.

Strength: Medium

Latte

Taste Profile:

Mild and creamy with more milk than cappuccino. Smooth, slightly sweet, and less intense. Perfect for those who prefer a gentler coffee taste.

Strength: Mild

Mocha

Taste Profile:

Sweet and indulgent with chocolate notes blending with espresso. Creamy, dessert-like experience with rich cocoa undertones.

Strength: Mild-Medium

Macchiato

Taste Profile:

Strong espresso "marked" with a dollop of foamed milk. Bold coffee flavor with just a hint of creaminess to soften the intensity.

Strength: Strong

Flat White

Taste Profile:

Velvety smooth with microfoam milk. Strong espresso flavor shines through the silky texture. More coffee-forward than a latte.

Strength: Medium-Strong

Cold Brew

Taste Profile:

Smooth, sweet, and low in acidity. Steeped in cold water for 12+ hours, resulting in a naturally sweet, chocolatey flavor without bitterness.

Strength: Medium
